## What I Build and Explain

I create content and repositories at the intersection of **Data Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Analytics, and Business Impact**.

My signature approach:

```text
Industry Application → Adoption & Evolution → Research Foundation → Practical Build → Impact & Takeaways
```

This means every project is designed to answer five questions:

1. Where is this concept used in the real world?
2. How did it evolve into mainstream adoption?
3. What research or technical foundation introduced it?
4. How can it be implemented with working code?
5. What business value does it create?
